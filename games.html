At Rap Yap, we believe that public speaking skills can be developed through practicing freestyle rap. We aim to provide a fun and educational place for speakers of all ages to hone their skills through playing online games. Check out what we have developed below!
Rhyme Game
Sharpen up those rhyming skills, and get better at using a more diverse vocabulary in your speech. This game requires you to come up with words that rhyme with a prompt -- the faster the better!
